# Requirements Document

## Introduction

InternshipKaro is a hands-on, real-world experience platform that connects students and early-career professionals with actual industry projects. This is NOT a training course or theory-based program. It's an objective-driven, practical work platform that provides real deliverables, portfolio-building opportunities, and industry-recognized certifications through completion of actual project work.

## Glossary

- **Platform**: The InternshipKaro web application
- **Participant**: A user working on real projects through the platform (students, early-career professionals)
- **Project**: A real-world industry project with specific objectives and deliverables
- **Domain**: A technology or industry area (Web Development, Data Science, etc.)
- **Objective**: A specific, measurable goal within a project that must be completed
- **Deliverable**: A tangible output or result from completing project objectives
- **Certification**: Industry-recognized credential awarded upon successful project completion
- **Partner_Company**: Organizations providing real projects and validation
- **Portfolio**: Collection of completed project deliverables showcasing practical skills
- **Experience_Track**: Structured progression through projects within a specific domain

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Hero Section and Value Proposition

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to immediately understand that InternshipKaro offers real-world project experience, so that I can quickly assess if this platform meets my career development needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor lands on the homepage, THE Platform SHALL display a compelling headline focused on real-world experience and hands-on industry projects
2. THE Platform SHALL include subtext highlighting objectives, portfolio building, and industry-recognized certifications
3. THE Platform SHALL feature a prominent "Start Your Journey" call-to-action button
4. THE Platform SHALL clearly communicate that this is NOT a training program but real project work
5. THE Platform SHALL use professional, confident, and credible messaging throughout

### Requirement 2: Core Value Propositions Display

**User Story:** As a potential participant, I want to understand the key benefits of the platform, so that I can make an informed decision about joining.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL display core value propositions with appropriate icons/cards including:
   - Objective-based work on real projects
   - Industry-recognized certifications
   - Real project experience for portfolios
   - Practical skills over theory
2. WHEN displaying value propositions, THE Platform SHALL use clean, modern design with minimal but impactful animations
3. THE Platform SHALL ensure all messaging emphasizes hands-on experience over theoretical learning
4. THE Platform SHALL include hover effects and subtle transitions for enhanced user experience
5. THE Platform SHALL maintain visual hierarchy that guides users toward key benefits

### Requirement 3: Platform Statistics and Credibility

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see proof of the platform's success and scale, so that I can trust the quality and legitimacy of the experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL prominently display key statistics:
   - 10K+ Projects Completed
   - 500+ Partner Companies
   - 95% Completion Rate
   - 50+ Tech Domains
2. THE Platform SHALL present statistics in a visually appealing format with appropriate icons
3. THE Platform SHALL ensure statistics are prominently placed to build immediate credibility
4. THE Platform SHALL update statistics regularly to maintain accuracy
5. THE Platform SHALL use these metrics throughout the site to reinforce credibility

### Requirement 4: About Section and Platform Explanation

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to understand exactly what InternshipKaro is and how it differs from traditional training, so that I have clear expectations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL explain InternshipKaro as a hands-on experience platform in clear, simple language
2. THE Platform SHALL explicitly state: "This is NOT a training program"
3. THE Platform SHALL emphasize real objectives, real deliverables, and certification on completion
4. THE Platform SHALL differentiate the platform from online courses and theoretical programs
5. THE Platform SHALL use zero corporate jargon and maintain an easy-to-understand tone

### Requirement 5: How It Works Process

**User Story:** As a potential participant, I want to understand the step-by-step process, so that I know what to expect when I join.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL display a clear 4-step process:
   - Choose a tech domain
   - Get assigned real projects
   - Complete objectives & deliverables
   - Earn industry-recognized certification
2. THE Platform SHALL use a step-based layout with visual progression indicators
3. THE Platform SHALL include brief descriptions for each step
4. THE Platform SHALL maintain visual consistency across all process steps
5. THE Platform SHALL make the process appear straightforward and achievable

### Requirement 6: Why Choose This Platform Section

**User Story:** As a potential participant, I want to understand the unique advantages of this platform, so that I can compare it with other options.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL highlight key differentiators:
   - Hands-on industry experience
   - Objective-based structured work
   - Portfolio-ready deliverables
   - Industry exposure and networking
   - Recognized practical experience
2. THE Platform SHALL present these advantages in a scannable format with icons or visual elements
3. THE Platform SHALL emphasize practical outcomes over theoretical knowledge
4. THE Platform SHALL use testimonials or proof points to support each advantage
5. THE Platform SHALL maintain consistent messaging about real-world value

### Requirement 7: Technology Domains Showcase

**User Story:** As a participant, I want to explore available technology domains, so that I can choose the area that aligns with my career goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL display domains in a cards/grid layout including:
   - Web Development
   - Mobile App Development
   - Data Science & Machine Learning
   - DevOps & Cloud
   - UI/UX Design
   - Cybersecurity
2. WHEN displaying domain cards, THE Platform SHALL include levels, objectives count, and key technologies
3. THE Platform SHALL include a "Start Experience" CTA on each domain card
4. THE Platform SHALL provide brief descriptions of what participants will work on in each domain
5. THE Platform SHALL allow filtering or sorting of domains based on difficulty or popularity

### Requirement 8: Testimonials and Success Stories

**User Story:** As a potential participant, I want to see success stories from alumni, so that I can understand the potential career impact.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL feature alumni success stories highlighting career transformation through hands-on projects
2. THE Platform SHALL include specific details about projects completed and career outcomes achieved
3. THE Platform SHALL use authentic testimonials with photos and verifiable details
4. THE Platform SHALL rotate testimonials to showcase diverse success stories across different domains
5. THE Platform SHALL include metrics like salary increases, job placements, or skill improvements

### Requirement 9: Final Call-to-Action Section

**User Story:** As a visitor ready to join, I want a compelling final push to start my journey, so that I can easily take the next step.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL encourage users to join thousands of successful tech professionals
2. THE Platform SHALL reinforce credibility using placement rate, partner companies, and satisfaction rate
3. THE Platform SHALL include a prominent "Start Your Journey" or similar CTA button
4. THE Platform SHALL create urgency without being pushy or using false scarcity
5. THE Platform SHALL provide multiple ways to get started (browse domains, contact, etc.)

### Requirement 10: User Interface and Experience

**User Story:** As a user, I want a fast, responsive, and visually appealing website, so that I can easily navigate and understand the platform's value.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL implement modern, professional design with clean tech aesthetic
2. THE Platform SHALL be fully responsive and mobile-optimized
3. THE Platform SHALL load quickly with optimized images and minimal JavaScript
4. THE Platform SHALL include smooth scroll, hover effects, and subtle transitions
5. THE Platform SHALL maintain clear visual hierarchy throughout all sections
6. THE Platform SHALL use strong CTAs throughout the user journey
7. THE Platform SHALL feel like a legitimate industry platform, not an online course site

### Requirement 11: Project Assignment and Management

**User Story:** As a participant, I want to be assigned real industry projects with clear objectives, so that I can work on meaningful deliverables that build my portfolio.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a participant selects a domain, THE Platform SHALL assign appropriate real-world projects based on skill level
2. THE Platform SHALL provide clear project briefs with specific objectives and expected deliverables
3. THE Platform SHALL track project progress through objective completion rather than time spent
4. THE Platform SHALL provide project templates and guidelines that mirror industry standards
5. THE Platform SHALL ensure all projects result in portfolio-worthy deliverables

### Requirement 12: Industry Partnership Integration

**User Story:** As a participant, I want to work on projects from real companies, so that I gain authentic industry experience and networking opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL maintain partnerships with 500+ companies providing real project work
2. THE Platform SHALL validate that all projects come from legitimate industry sources
3. THE Platform SHALL provide opportunities for participants to interact with partner company representatives
4. THE Platform SHALL showcase partner company logos and testimonials for credibility
5. THE Platform SHALL facilitate potential hiring connections between participants and partner companies

### Requirement 13: Certification and Portfolio System

**User Story:** As a participant, I want to earn industry-recognized certifications and build a strong portfolio, so that I can demonstrate my practical skills to employers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a participant completes all project objectives, THE Platform SHALL issue industry-recognized certifications
2. THE Platform SHALL automatically compile completed deliverables into a professional portfolio
3. THE Platform SHALL provide portfolio templates optimized for different domains and career levels
4. THE Platform SHALL enable easy sharing of portfolios and certifications on professional networks
5. THE Platform SHALL maintain verification systems for employers to validate certifications

### Requirement 14: Progress Tracking and Objective Management

**User Story:** As a participant, I want to track my progress through clear objectives, so that I understand what I need to accomplish and can measure my advancement.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL break down projects into specific, measurable objectives
2. THE Platform SHALL provide a dashboard showing objective completion status and overall progress
3. THE Platform SHALL send notifications and reminders for upcoming objective deadlines
4. THE Platform SHALL provide feedback and validation when objectives are completed
5. THE Platform SHALL allow participants to see their progression path within their chosen domain

### Requirement 15: Quality Assurance and Project Validation

**User Story:** As a participant, I want assurance that my completed work meets industry standards, so that I can confidently showcase my deliverables.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL implement quality review processes for all completed deliverables
2. THE Platform SHALL provide industry-standard criteria and rubrics for project evaluation
3. THE Platform SHALL offer feedback and revision opportunities before final certification
4. THE Platform SHALL maintain high completion rates (95%) through appropriate project scoping
5. THE Platform SHALL ensure all certified work meets professional portfolio standards
